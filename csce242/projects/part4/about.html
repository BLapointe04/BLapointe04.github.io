Our Story

We started as two friends cooking smash burgers on a backyard griddle. Word spread, lines formed, and The Burger Tavern was born. We keep things simple: fresh beef, toasted buns, crispy edges, and flavor that speaks for itself.

From day one, we focused on consistency and care. If we wouldn’t serve it to friends and family, it doesn’t leave the kitchen.

Fresh Ingredients

We grind our beef fresh, slice our toppings daily, and make sauces in-house. Our buns are baked locally so they’re soft, warm, and ready for that toast on the griddle.

  • Fresh, never frozen beef
  • House sauces (yes, the Tavern sauce is real)
  • Locally baked buns

What We Care About

  • Keep it simple: a short menu done well.
  • Make it fresh: cooked to order, no shortcuts.
  • Treat people right: friendly, fast, and fair.

FAQ

Do you take large orders?

Yes. For a dozen or more meals, send us a note on the Contact page and we’ll set it up.

Do you have veggie options?

We have a Veggie Garden burger and can make most items without dairy on request.

What’s the best first order?

Classic Smash + curly fries + a side of Tavern sauce. Can’t go wrong.
