Our Story

We started as two friends chasing the perfect smash burger. No fluff, no complicated menu—just quality meat, fresh toppings, and a griddle that never sleeps.

Today we serve the food we like to eat on our days off. If it isn’t tasty or consistent, it doesn’t make the board.

Our Values

  • Keep it simple and honest.
  • Make it fresh and fast.
  • Treat guests like regulars from day one.
