Otters are playful aquatic mammals known for their agility in water and their thick fur Otters are cute but there are also different types of otters, the cute and cuddly ones we all know are the Sea Otters. There are different type of otters are the River Otter and Giant otter being the most common types in the wild. All otters have fur coats matching their respective environment and climate as otters wouldnt want a super thick coat while living in warmer climates. Otters are natural predator while also being prey to other creatures such as great white sharks, orcas, bald eagles, coyotes,and even bears!

Otters are found in many parts of the world, including North America, Europe, Asia, and Africa. They are social animals and often live in groups called rafts or families. These different type of otters all look different, but they still share qualities such as their use of tools! These cute creatures know how to utilize rocks and will smash open things to gather food and storing things in their pouches. Although otters look and act very cute they are not friendly to people in most scenarios
