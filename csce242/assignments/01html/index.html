Heres some things about Otters!

Otters are playful aquatic mammals known for their agility in water and their thick fur Otters are cute but there are also different types of otters, the cute and cuddly ones we all know are the Sea Otters, these cute cuddly creatures survive by cracking shells on rocks and using their thick fure to stay in the water for long times. Different type of otters are the River Otter and Gioant otter being the most common types in the wild.

Otters are found in many parts of the world, including North America, Europe, Asia, and Africa. They are social animals and often live in groups called rafts or families.
